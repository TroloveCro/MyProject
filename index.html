FAQ:

1. What if I fail test in class or on the road?
If you fail test on road or in class, application will be closed and you must pay another one.
Our process and Los Santos road and vehicle laws must be strict in all segments and we does not tolerate
mistakes. To avoid closing application our team will prepare candidate for best results.

1. What is conditions of passing.
conditions of passing are tolerance limits. If you have more then one wrong answers you will fail test in class.
This is only condition for class test, but conditions of passing on the road are more then just one wrong answer.
On road test you need to drive less then 30mph in city, obey road and traffic rules,
avoid damaging school vehicle or other vehicles and show learned parking skills.

I want to get driver license.

To get your driving licence you need to complete next tasks.
If you successfully complete all this tasks you will be able to get your documents.

  1. Complete road and traffic test in class.
  2. Drive 30 hours on road with your insturctor.
  3. Complete driving test on road.
